Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The task of a system developer is to design sustainable and user-friendly digital systems. In this program you will learn how to model, develop, organize and analyze digital information systems for various applications such as web or mobile applications. Information system development is a growing need in society, where new channels and media will make digitized information available to more and more people. Topics included in the program are: computer science, information technology, programming methodology and information security, all with focus on modeling, construction and analysis of digital systems. The teaching is based on project-driven learning where projects can be carried out in collaboration with the business community or other external actors so that you as a student get training in the future professional role.
